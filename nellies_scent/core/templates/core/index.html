Our Story

Born from a passion
for fine fragrance

Nellie's Scent was born in Calabar from a love of elegant fragrance composition. After moving to Abuja, we extended our reach — bringing sophisticated scents to the Nigerian market, curated for the modern woman.

Each fragrance is handpicked and tested to meet our high standards. We believe a great perfume is more than a scent — it's a statement, a memory, and a part of your story.

From Arabian florals to luxurious western blends, our collection celebrates confidence, elegance, and individuality.
